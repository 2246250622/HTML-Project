Q&A

What is the Club Premium?

The Club Premium is BaBy Gaming's shareholders club. Bespoke events are organized for our members in our Partner institutions.

 

Who can become a Club Premium member?

To become a member, as long as you are at least 18 years old and need to pay 400HKD every month

 

How do I enroll in the BaBy Gaming club?

To enroll, simply complete the form and the sworn declaration.

 

What should I do if my email address has changed?

Emil us (123456@gmail.com) and attach your old also new email address.

 

What should I do if I want to participate in an event?

To be our member and join our Discord group.We will update and announce the event time and location from time to time.

 

I cannot register for an event. Why?

Because you over 18 years old or incorrect Emaill address / HKID

 

Does the Club Premium organise transportation to the event venue?

Absolutely not.

 

How are participants selected?

If the quota is full, just decision by random.
